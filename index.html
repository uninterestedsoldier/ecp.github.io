  • Barbecuing

    • Fancy cooking up a feast and dining with fantastic views of the sea? You can hold a barbecue with your friends and family at the one of the 80 BBQ pits located across the entire East Coast Park. To book a barbecue pit, click here.



  • Cycling & Skating

    • With a Park Connector Network that stretches about 12.5km along the south-eastern coastline, East Coast Park is a cyclist’s and skater’s haven. Check out Learner’s and Advanced Circuit at Cyclist Park, Area D. If you like skateboarding, stop by the Xtreme SkatePark@East Coast to try out the ramps there
